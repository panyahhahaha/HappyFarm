📖 How to Play (วิธีเล่น)

1. Plant Seeds: Select a seed from your inventory and click on any empty soil plot.

2. Water Crops: Click on a growing crop. Solve a fun Math or English question to water it and speed up growth!

3. Harvest & Sell: Click on fully grown crops to harvest. Sell harvested crops in the Harvest Stock sidebar to earn gold coins!

4. Learning Barn: Visit the Learning Barn to answer quizzes and earn direct coin rewards.
